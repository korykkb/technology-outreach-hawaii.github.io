Programs


Explore technology programs created and lead by our teams of students. We believe that having a background in technology is an essential skill to have in today's society. Programs are geared towards different age groups and specific interests. Learn more about our unqiue programs below.

ICSpark

ICSpark is a Registered Independent Organization (RIO) at the University of Hawaii. ICSpark began with a desire to offer coding programs to students at a younger age. Our founding undergraduate students believed that early education would be invaluable to kickstart a career in CS, and in developing the critical thinking and skills necessary to succeed in any sector in the 21st century.
